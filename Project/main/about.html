About Us

The Voltage & Current Monitor project is designed to provide real-time data monitoring for voltage and current levels in various systems. Our mission is to empower users with accurate data for better energy management, safety, and performance optimization.

We aim to deliver a reliable, user-friendly solution for tracking electrical metrics in homes, industries, and institutions.
